My Simple White Sauce Recipe

This is just a simple white sauce to add in my fried chicken


Ingredients

  • Butter - 50mg
  • All Purpose Flour - 50mg
  • Heavy Cream or All Purpose Cream - 250mg
  • Pinch Salt
  • Pinch Pepper
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Heat the pan for 5 seconds
  2. Put the Butter and steer until it melts
  3. Put the Flour and whisk it until the color is golden
  4. Add the cream and continue to whisk until it is think and creamy
  5. Add salt and pepper
  6. Add oregano and turn off the heat
  7. Done!

Optional

You can add onions and garlic to it. Just add it on step 2
